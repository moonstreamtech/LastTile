Last Tile is a calm, focused merge puzzle. You start on a small active region inside a locked frame and grow the board one ring at a time as you climb tier by tier. Every merge costs a turn. Slides and swaps are free, but the moved tile is locked until its value changes. Splits give you a way out when no merge is left.

The run ends when no adjacent matching pair remains. Play at your own pace. There is no timer.

How to play
▸ Tap a tile, then tap an adjacent matching tile to merge.
▸ Or drag any tile onto an adjacent matching tile to merge directly.
▸ Double-tap a tile (value 2 or higher) to split it into two halves.
▸ Drag a tile one step onto an empty cell to slide, or onto a non-matching neighbor to swap.

Features
▸ Growing board — start at 5×5 inside a 7×7 frame; expand to 7×7, 9×9, 11×11 and beyond as you unlock the frame.
▸ Value-tier frame unlocks — reaching 64, 128, 256 and higher tiers opens 1 to 6 random locked cells at a time.
▸ Combo system — chain merges to multiply your score; sliding or swapping resets the combo, so plan when to spend a slide.
▸ Hazards — fire, ice and poison can infect a tile for a few turns. Burn them off with a high-tier merge nearby, or wait them out and the tile returns with the same value (poison softens it).
▸ On-device leaderboard — your best ten runs are saved locally with score, turn count and final board size.
▸ Auto-save — close the app any time. The exact same board, score and combo come back when you return.
▸ Fully offline, single player — no account, no login, no internet required.
▸ Game state stays on your device. The only data leaving the device is your final score (when you sign in to Google Play Games for the global leaderboard) and the standard ad signals used by the bottom banner.
