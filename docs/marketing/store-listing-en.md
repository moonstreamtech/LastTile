## Full Description

> Limit: 4000 characters. Shown on the Play Store app page.

```
Last Tile is a calm merge puzzle with real strategic depth. Merge tiles, climb tiers, fight off hazards, and grow the board until no match remains. No timers, no pressure — just thoughtful moves at your own pace.

Every successful merge is a turn. The board starts as a small active region inside a locked frame and keeps growing as you reach value-tier milestones. Slides and swaps are free, splits get you out of a lockout, and the run ends only when no adjacent matching pair remains anywhere on the board.

Then the hazards arrive. Fire, Ice and Poison tiles freeze the cells they sit on. Burn them off with high-tier merges, wait some of them out, or spend a Shield to clean them and keep the underlying value. Every move counts.

FEATURES

▸ Calm gameplay — no timers, no pressure, no forced sessions. Play one quick run or settle in for a long session.
▸ Growing board — start at 5×5, expand to 7×7, 9×9 and beyond as you climb tiers.
▸ Four deliberate actions — tap-tap merge, drag-to-merge, double-tap split, one-step slide or swap.
▸ Hazard system — Fire, Ice and Poison tiles add real strategic depth without ever feeling cheap.
▸ Shield mechanic — spend a shield to cleanse a hazard and keep the underlying tile. Out of shields? Watch a single optional rewarded video for three more.
▸ Auto-save — close the app any time. The exact same board, score and combo come back when you return.
▸ Local top-ten leaderboard — your best ten runs, stored on-device.
▸ Optional global leaderboard — sign in to Google Play Games to compete worldwide. Sign-in is fully optional; the rest of the game works without it.
▸ Multi-language — English and Turkish at launch.

PRIVACY AND RESPECT

Last Tile is free to install and play, supported by a single non-intrusive banner ad and optional rewarded videos. There is no required account. There is no analytics SDK. Permissions are limited to the network access that ads and leaderboards need. The full privacy policy is available in-app and at moonstreamtech.com/LastTile/privacy-policy/.

For puzzle players who want a quiet, focused session — no flashing rewards, no forced timers, no required login. Just merge, plan and protect your last tile.
```

Keep it under 4000 characters when pasting. The block above is well
under the limit and leaves room for small edits.
