Last Tile is a single-player puzzle game for Android.

Data we collect

Last Tile does not collect personal data on its own servers. The game runs fully on your device, with one optional online feature described below.

Data stored on your device

The following game state is stored locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences and never leaves the device:

  • Current run state (board, score, turn, combo, unlocked frame cells).
  • On-device leaderboard (your best ten runs: score, turn count, board size, timestamp).
  • Settings.

You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app.

Online Leaderboard (Google Play Games Services)

When you achieve a high score, your score may be submitted to Google Play Games Services for inclusion in our global leaderboard. The submitted data includes your score, your Google Play Games player ID, and your display name if you choose to make it visible. This is governed by Google's Play Games Services Privacy Policy.

You can opt out by signing out of Google Play Games. The local leaderboard will continue to work without sign-in.

Third-party services

Google Play Games Services — used solely to host the optional global leaderboard. Google handles all account, identity, and storage on its side; Last Tile only forwards the numeric score. See Google's Play Games Services Privacy Policy for details.

If a future version adds an ad network, analytics, or any other third-party service, this policy and the in-app data safety disclosure will be updated before that version is published.

Children

The game is family-friendly, has no user-generated content, no chat, no purchases, and no ads in this version. It is suitable for all ages 7 and above. Children using Google Play Games are covered by Google's family account controls.
